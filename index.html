About Us

Empower Sustainable Energy (formerly Empower Electrical) has built a strong reputation in energy infrastructure and electrical services over the past two decades. In response to the growing need for equitable access to energy, we have evolved from a for-profit enterprise into a non-profit organization committed to developing sustainable energy solutions for underserved communities.

As leaders in mini-grid and microgrid development, we focus on delivering reliable, decentralized energy solutions that align with SDG 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy). Our experience in electrical engineering, energy management, and infrastructure enables us to empower communities with scalable, community-driven energy systems. In a time when South Africa faces significant energy challenges, we are at the forefront of providing impactful, sustainable energy solutions that advance the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
